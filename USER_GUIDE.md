# TravelLedger Pro - User Guide

Welcome to **TravelLedger Pro**, your complete accounting and management software for travel agencies. This guide will help you understand how to use the system in simple steps.

---

## 1. Getting Started
When you open the app, you will see the **Dashboard**. This gives you a quick look at your total balances, recent transactions, and financial health.

## 2. Managing Your Accounts (Chart of Accounts)
The **Chart of Accounts (COA)** is where you keep all your account heads.
- **Add New Account**: Click the "Add New Account" button. Give it a name, code, and type (like Cash, Bank, Expense, or Income).
- **Edit Account**: Click on any account to change its details.
- **View Ledger**: Click on an account name to see its full transaction history (Ledger).

## 3. Customers and Vendors
- **Customers**: People or companies you sell to. You can see their total balance and download their ledger as a PDF.
- **Vendors**: Suppliers or airlines you buy from. You can track how much you owe them.

## 4. Recording Transactions (Vouchers)
Vouchers are used to record every money movement.
- **Receipt Voucher**: Use this when you **receive money** from a customer.
  - *Paid From*: The customer who gave you money.
  - *Received In*: Your Bank or Cash account.
- **Payment Voucher**: Use this when you **pay money** for expenses or to vendors.
  - *Paid From*: Your Bank or Cash account.
  - *Paid To*: The expense head or vendor you are paying.
- **Service Vouchers**: Special forms for specific travel services:
  - **Ticket Voucher**: For airline ticket sales.
  - **Hotel Voucher**: For hotel bookings.
  - **Visa Voucher**: For visa processing fees.
  - **Transport Voucher**: For car or bus rentals.

## 5. General Ledger
The **General Ledger** shows every transaction in the system in order of date and time. You can filter it by account name to see exactly where money is going.

## 6. Reports
The **Reports** section helps you understand your business performance.
- **Trial Balance**: Checks if your total debits match your total credits.
- **Profit & Loss**: Shows if you are making money or losing it.
- **Balance Sheet**: Shows your total assets and liabilities.

## 7. Control Panel (Settings)
The **Control Panel** is for administrators.
- **Branding**: Upload your company logo and change the company name.
- **Financials**: Set the default exchange rate (e.g., PKR to SAR).
- **Security**: Create new user accounts for your staff.
- **Disaster Recovery (Backup)**: 
  - **Automatic Daily Backup**: Enable this in the Disaster tab to have the system automatically download a backup every day at 12:00 AM (or the first time you open the app each day).
  - **Interval Backup**: Set a custom interval (e.g., every 6 hours) for automatic backups. The timer resets after each successful backup.
  - **Download Backup**: Manually save your data to your computer in **Excel** or **JSON** format.
  - **Restore**: If you lose data, you can upload your backup file to get everything back.

## 8. Tips for Success
- **Daily Backups**: Always download an Excel backup at the end of every day.
- **Double Check**: Ensure "Paid From" and "Received In" accounts are correct before saving a voucher.
- **Search**: Use the search bars in the Ledger or Vouchers list to find old transactions quickly.
